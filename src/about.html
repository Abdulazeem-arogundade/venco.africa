Welcome to Venco

Your partner in transforming living experiences in residential and commercial communities across Africa. At Venco, we're dedicated to simplifying property management through our cutting-edge technology platform.

Our Story

Venco is a technology company that provides solutions to enhance living experiences in residential and commercial communities in Africa, through our all-in-one technology platform that manages collections, service charge administration, utility vending, visitor access and other services associated with multi-unit property developments across Africa.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To empower communities with innovative technology for seamless living experiences.

Our Mission: To enhance the quality of life in residential and commercial properties through comprehensive, user-friendly solutions.

Our Solution

Venco offers a suite of solutions designed to streamline property management:

  1. Collections Management: Efficiently handle financial transactions.
  2. Service Charge Administration: Simplify billing and payment processes.
  3. Utility Vending: Hassle-free utility payments.
  4. Visitor Access: Secure and convenient access management.
  5. Other Services: Tailored solutions for unique community needs.

Our solutions have transformed countless communities, improving efficiency, reducing costs, and enhancing the overall living experience.
